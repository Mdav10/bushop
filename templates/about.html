About BuShop

Our Mission

BuShop is a web platform that allows people in Burundi to browse products, place orders, pay through Lumicash, and become permanent customers whose purchasing history can be tracked by the business owner.

What We Offer

  • Easy product browsing and searching
  • Secure payments via Lumicash
  • Order tracking and history
  • Customer dashboard for order management
  • Real-time notifications
